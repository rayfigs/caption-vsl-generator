My mother called again.

She wanted to know if my daughter is "keeping up" with the other kids. I told her she built a water filtration system last week. My mother said that's nice, but does she know her times tables.

I didn't argue. I've stopped arguing.

Because I watched my daughter walk into school this morning like she owned the building. Not nervous. Not dragging her feet. Actually excited.

Seven years old. Actually excited to go to school on a Monday.

I second-guess the cost every month. Every single month. And then I see her like that, and I remember.

We made the right call.

Hold the line.
