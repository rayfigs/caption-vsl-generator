We moved to Cluj for my husband's job. We gave ourselves six months to figure out the school situation.

Finding an English-medium school here that didn't feel like an afterthought — that took a while.

The first time I walked into Colina, my daughter held my hand the whole tour. At every other school, she had hidden behind me.

She started two months ago. Last week she taught me a Romanian word she learned from a friend.

I don't know how long we'll be in Romania. But whatever happens next, she'll carry this with her.
