I read the research. I know what project-based learning is. I had the whole conversation with myself before I even started looking at schools.

And then I had to have it with my mother. And my mother-in-law. And the pediatrician. And basically everyone at our daughter's birthday party.

The question is always the same: "But will she learn maths?"

She's seven. She designed a water filtration system last month as part of a science unit. She can explain how clean water gets from a mountain to a tap.

But sure. Flash cards. Let's talk about flash cards.

The cost is real. Every month I feel it. And every month I watch my daughter walk into that building like she owns the place.

Worth it.
