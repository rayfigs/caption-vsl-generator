Your best front desk person has been with you for six years.

She knows everything. How you like things done, what to say when insurance pushes back, how to handle a last-minute cancellation.

And the day she leaves, that knowledge walks out with her.

Most practices run on tribal knowledge. Information that lives in people's heads, not in the business.

When that person goes, you spend the next three months rebuilding what they knew.

SOPs fix this. Not because people won't leave — they will — but because when they do, the knowledge stays.
